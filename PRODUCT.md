# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Developers, prompt authors, and agent-curious builders who want strong starting instructions for common software projects. They are usually trying to get from an idea to a working scaffold quickly, while still keeping enough taste and judgment in the loop to avoid vague AI-generated mush.

## Product Purpose

Prompts for Mere Mortals is a public prompt catalog for opinionated AI coding starters. It packages each starter as an approachable incantation: pick a stack, inspect the tradeoffs, copy the prompt, and begin with stronger defaults than a blank chat box.

Success means visitors immediately understand the site's point of view, find a relevant prompt without friction, and feel invited to improve the grimoire through pull requests when a spell or stack can be sharper.

## Brand Personality

Playful, arcane, and opinionated. The tone should feel like a friendly grimoire maintained by practical builders: tongue-in-cheek, clear about tradeoffs, and never generic SaaS.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI tools directories, sterile gradient SaaS landing pages, terminal-only hacker cosplay, and overly serious editorial magazine treatments. Do not flatten the site into a neutral documentation index. Do not let the magical language obscure what each prompt actually does.

## Design Principles

- Keep the grimoire visible. The magical metaphor should shape labels, affordances, and contribution language without making actions hard to understand.
- Prefer opinionated clarity. Explain what each prompt is for, what stack it chooses, and why a builder would pick it.
- Make contribution feel ceremonial, not bureaucratic. Inviting a pull request should feel like petitioning a council, while still giving users a direct next step.
- Preserve the hand-built catalog feel. Chunky outlines, playful type, strong color, and tactile controls are part of the identity.
- Respect the builder's time. Search, filtering, copying, and reading the full prompt should stay fast and obvious.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Assume WCAG AA as the baseline. Maintain readable contrast, keyboard-operable controls, clear focus states, semantic headings, screen-reader-friendly status updates, and reduced-motion alternatives for decorative animation. Whimsy should never depend on color alone or make core actions harder to parse.
